About Us

What is ACM?

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and a profession.

Our Mission

Fostering innovation, continuous learning, and collaboration among computer science students. We bridge the gap between academic curriculum and industry demands.

Chapter Goals

Knowledge sharing, technical workshops, global networking, and building a tight-knit community for aspiring technologists.

"ACM Student Chapter of ACE Engineering College was established to create a supportive space for students passionate about computing and technology."
